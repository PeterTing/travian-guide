    GetterTools.com provides 14 professional free tools for Travian Legends players, organized into two main categories: "Most Used Tools" and "Calculators". They cover everything from early-game farm management, resource calculations, to late-game tactical coordination and defense planning. Developed by experienced Travian players, these tools can significantly improve your gaming efficiency and decision-making quality. Whether you're a newcomer or a veteran, using these tools wisely will dramatically enhance your gameplay experience.

    Essential for alliance leaders. Manage all member troops, plan attacks, coordinate defense. Set up your group early.

    Battle Reports Analyzer
    訪問工具
    ★☆☆☆☆ 初級

    Convert in-game battle reports into shareable links. Supports hiding attacker/defender info, statistics, battle map, and multi-report packs.

    Hide attacker info when posting on forums for privacy. Statistics feature analyzes loss ratios.

    View server statistics, player/alliance rankings, growth curves, population rankings. Search detailed info for any player or alliance.

    • Player name or alliance name

    Regularly monitor competitor growth trends to identify potential threats and farm targets.

    Search Inactives

    不活躍玩家搜尋器
    Inactive Player Finder
    訪問工具
    ★☆☆☆☆ 初級
    用途
    搜尋指定範圍內的不活躍玩家（死羊），是建立農場清單的核心工具。
    Find inactive players within a specified radius — the core tool for building farm lists.
    輸入參數

    • 中心座標、搜尋半徑、人口過濾、7日成長過濾

    • Center coordinates, search radius, population filter, 7-day growth filter

    💡 建議

    建議每 6 小時掃描一次，新伺服器初期死羊較少，中期開始大量出現。

    Scan every 6 hours. Early server has fewer inactives; they increase significantly mid-game.

    Crop Tool

    ★★☆☆☆ 中級

    • Copy-paste from Travian marketplace (requires login)

    Defense Tables

    防禦協調表
    Defense Tables
    訪問工具
    ★★☆☆☆ 中級
    用途
    快速建立防禦請求表。防禦者可看到自己各村到被攻擊村莊的行軍時間，一鍵發送防禦部隊。
    Quickly create defense request tables. Defenders can see travel times from their villages to the attacked village and send troops with one click.
    輸入參數

    • 被攻擊村莊座標、攻擊到達時間

    • Attacked village coordinates, attack arrival time

    💡 建議

    被攻擊時立即建立防禦表分享給盟友，比在聊天中請求防禦更有效率。

    Create a defense table immediately when attacked and share with allies — much more efficient than requesting defense in chat.

    攔截計算器
    Interception Calculator
    訪問工具
    ★★★★★ 進階
    用途

    Calculate optimal interception timing and position. Plan troop dispatch times for intercepting incoming attacks.

    Culture Points Calculator
    訪問工具
    ★☆☆☆☆ 初級
    用途
    計算到達下一個開村門檻所需時間，規劃擴張時間線。
    Calculate time to next village expansion threshold, plan your expansion timeline.

    • Current CP, daily CP production

    Essential from game start. Precisely calculate when you can settle 2nd and 3rd villages.

    Technology Calculator

    科技計算器
    Technology Calculator
    訪問工具
    ★★☆☆☆ 中級
    用途
    計算研發和升級兵種所需的精確資源和時間，規劃科技升級順序。
    Calculate exact resources and time needed for research and troop upgrades, plan technology upgrade order.
    輸入參數

    • 種族、兵種、當前等級

    • Tribe, unit type, current level

    💡 建議

    決定研發順序前先計算成本效益，避免浪費資源在低效升級上。

    Calculate cost-effectiveness before deciding research order to avoid wasting resources on inefficient upgrades.

    💰

    NPC Calculator

    NPC 交易計算器
    NPC Calculator
    訪問工具
    ★☆☆☆☆ 初級
    用途
    計算 NPC 商人的最佳資源交換比例，確保不浪費金幣。
    Calculate optimal resource exchange ratios for NPC traders. Ensure no gold is wasted.
    輸入參數

    • 當前各資源量、目標資源比例

    • Current resource amounts, target resource ratio

    💡 建議

    用金幣 NPC 前先計算，確認交換後的資源足夠你的建築/訓練需求。

    Calculate before using gold NPC to confirm exchanged resources meet your building/training needs.

    🏃

    Save Troops

    保兵工具
    Save Troops
    訪問工具
    ★★☆☆☆ 中級
    用途
    計算最佳的保兵策略。根據來襲時間計算部隊應何時出發逃避攻擊，以及何時安全返回。
    Calculate optimal troop-saving strategy. Based on incoming attack time, calculate when troops should depart to dodge and when they can safely return.
    輸入參數

    • 來襲時間、自己村莊座標、部隊速度

    • Incoming attack time, own village coordinates, troop speed

    💡 建議

    被打時優先保兵而非硬扛，計算好出發時間讓部隊在攻擊到達前離開。

    When under attack, prioritize saving troops over tanking. Time departure so troops leave before the attack lands.

    📐

    Path Calculator

    路徑計算器
    Path Calculator
    訪問工具
    ★☆☆☆☆ 初級
    用途
    計算部隊從 A 點到 B 點的精確行軍時間，支援競技場和英雄加速。
    Calculate precise troop march time from point A to B, supports Tournament Square and hero speed bonuses.
    輸入參數

    • 出發/目的地座標、部隊類型、競技場等級

    • Departure/destination coordinates, troop type, TS level

    💡 建議

    掠奪時計算來回時間決定最佳頻率，防禦時計算援軍到達時間。

    Calculate round-trip time for optimal raid frequency; calculate reinforcement arrival for defense.

    🔬

    Path-Speed-TS

    進階速度反推計算器
    Advanced Speed Reverse Calculator
    訪問工具
    ★★★★★ 進階
    用途
    從來襲部隊的行軍時間反推對方的部隊速度和競技場等級。用於判斷來犯部隊是步兵還是騎兵、攻擊還是偵查。
    Reverse-calculate enemy troop speed and Tournament Square level from incoming travel time. Determine if incoming troops are infantry or cavalry, attack or scout.
    輸入參數

    • 攻擊方/目標座標、行軍時間、緩衝時間

    • Attacker/target coordinates, travel time, buffer time

    💡 建議

    防禦時的關鍵情報工具。知道對方兵種後才能決定要不要防、怎麼防。

    Critical intelligence tool for defense. Knowing enemy unit type lets you decide whether and how to defend.
